INSTALATION INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1 - Download The Apk And ZIP.
Step 2 - Install The Apk On Your Device.
Step 3 - Upon Installing,Go To The Google Play Store And Download "Pydroid3" App.
Step 4 - Upon Installing,Extract The ZIP File,If Your File Manager Cant Do This,I Recommend Downloading "ZaArchiver" App,Its From Google Play Store Too.
Step 5 - Upon Doing All Of That,Open The "Pydroid3" App,After That,Click On The Folder Icon,After That Click On The "Open" Button And Locate Your Extracted File,After That Click On The Folder That Appeared After The Extraction,After Clicking On The Folder And Opening It,There Will Be Another Folder So Click On It Too,After That,Click On The "main.py"File,After That,The File Will Be Opened In Your "Pydroid3" App,After That Click On The Play Button In The Bottom Right Corner,After That Click On The "_" Button And Thats It,Launch Your Game And Enjoy!
